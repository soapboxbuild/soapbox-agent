# Engagement Reference — 4th & Madison Decarbonization Plan

**Client / Owner:** JP Metro Asset Management
**Asset:** 4th & Madison (office, 1 building, ~83,000 m² GFA, built 2003, 22 floors, Metro City)
**Engagement type:** Decarbonization Plan (full engagement)

## Goal
Achieve compliance with the Metro City Building Performance Standard (BPS) ahead of the 2032 first-compliance deadline, while positioning the asset for a competitive disposition in ~10 years.

## Drivers
- **bps-compliance**: Metro City BPS, first compliance year 2032, cap 30 kgCO2e/m2/yr, fine $268/tCO2e over cap
- **asset-repositioning**: Owner wants a defensible decarbonization narrative ahead of a planned ~Year 10 disposition
- **equipment-end-of-life**: Central hydronic furnace (2003) and central chiller (2010) both approach end of expected service life within the hold period

## Primary target
bps-fine-avoidance — 30 (Metro City BPS cap, 30 kgCO2e/m2/yr, first compliance year 2032)

## Secondary targets
- percent — 20 (Owner-stated informal goal: at least 20% site carbon reduction from baseline within the hold period)

## Hold period
10 years

## Budget
no hard ceiling set at kickoff; owner asked for phased capital tied to equipment replacement cycles where possible

## Regulatory driver
Metro City Building Performance Standard (BPS): first compliance year 2032, cap 30 kgCO2e/m²/yr, fine $268/tCO2e over cap.
